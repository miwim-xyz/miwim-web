# Getting Started

## For Users

Miwim is currently in beta. Here's how to get started:

### Step 1: Join the Waitlist
Visit [miwim.io](/) and sign up for the waitlist. You'll receive:
- Priority access when we launch
- Airdrop eligibility for $MIWIM tokens
- Early beta invitations

### Step 2: Download the App (Beta)
Once invited to beta:
1. Download the Miwim Android app from the provided link
2. Create an account with your waitlist email
3. The app will automatically fetch your configuration

### Step 3: Connect
1. Open the app
2. Select your preferred region (or use auto-select)
3. Tap "Connect" — you're now routing through the Miwim decentralized network
