## What is Miwim?

Miwim will operate two product lines sharing one decentralized supply network:

- **Miwim Backbone** — Datacenter proxy nodes for consumers. Low-latency VPN alternative with monthly subscription pricing (~$8-15/mo).
- **Miwim Edge** — Residential IP proxy via real home broadband. For B2B/Pro users needing genuine residential IPs for e-commerce, data collection, and anti-detection (~$1-3/GB).

Both lines will be powered by the **$MIWIM token** — used for provider staking, user payment discounts, governance, and revenue buyback-and-burn.

## Why Decentralized?

Traditional proxy and VPN providers are centralized: a single company controls all servers, all user data, and all funds. When they shut down — users lose everything.

Miwim distributes infrastructure across independent providers worldwide. No single entity can shut down the network, seize user data, or block access.
